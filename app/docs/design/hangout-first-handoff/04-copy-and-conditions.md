## Nothing-day statements (the rotating set)
"Nothing days" are common and welcome — replace a single line with this set. **Rotate daily;
prefer a location/time-aware line when the data supports it, else an evergreen permission line;
never repeat the same line two days running.**

**Evergreen — permission & rest**
- Today's yours.
- No plans, on purpose.
- Nothing on the books. Good.
- The only plan is no plan.
- An open day. Leave it that way.
- Empty page, full day.
- A day to do nothing in particular.
- Stay in your pajamas a while.

**Being-there & presence**
- Wherever everyone drifts is the plan.
- See where the day takes you.
- Follow the good light.
- A slow one, together.
- Whatever the kids invent today.
- Be where your feet are.

**Spontaneity — a soft door, never a push**
- Something will turn up. It usually does.
- Leave room for the accidental.
- Wander first, decide later.
- Nothing planned — peek at what's nearby, or don't.

**Location / time-aware (surface only when the data fits)**
- Let the tide set the pace. *(ocean)*
- Beach until you're hungry. *(ocean)*
- Dock time. *(lake)*
- Let the trail decide. *(mountains)*
- Get lost on purpose. *(city)*
- Golden hour's at 7:12 — that's the only appointment. *(any, evening)*
- Coffee's the whole morning. *(any, morning)*
- Save it all for the sunset. *(any, afternoon)*
- Clear sky tonight — worth staying up for. *(desert / clear)*

## Location-adaptive conditions model
The home's "heartbeat" line and the Map's stay panel surface **3–4 conditions** — chosen for the
place. **Tide only near an ocean.** Never show a variable the location doesn't have.

| Place nature | Surface these (pick 3–4) |
|---|---|
| **Always available (fallback trio)** | `GOLDEN HOUR` · `SUNSET` · `FEELS-LIKE` (+ `DAYLIGHT LEFT` in the afternoon) |
| **Ocean / bay** | `TIDE` (going out/in + low/high time) · `WATER TEMP` · `GOLDEN/SUNSET` |
| **Lake / river** | `WATER TEMP` · `SUNSET` · (wind, if boating) |
| **Mountains / alpine** | `FIRST LIGHT` · `TEMP SWING` (cool AM) · `SUNSET` |
| **Desert / high UV** | `UV INDEX` · `SUNSET` · `STARS TONIGHT` (clear-sky) |
| **City** | `GOLDEN/SUNSET` · `FEELS-LIKE` · one civic beat ("quiet till 10," market day). *Transit frequency lives in the Map / Getting-there, not the calm home.* |
| **Cold season / snow** | `FIRST LIGHT` · `DAYLIGHT LEFT` · `FEELS-LIKE` |

**Rule.** Pick the 3–4 most relevant for the place + time of day. The readout is **ambient, never
a to-do** — it's the heartbeat line under "who's around," and the Map's stay panel.
