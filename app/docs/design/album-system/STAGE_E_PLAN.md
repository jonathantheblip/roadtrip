## 1. What the design is
Three chapters of a design handoff for the trip's photo album: an album that **organizes itself** (navigation, filters, best-of), a way to **finish the story of any past day or trip** after the fact, and how the family **learns a photo moved** (and moves one by hand). It is the Stage-E *surface* blueprint — the visible screens that sit on top of the plumbing Stages B, C, and D build.

## 2. How it maps to the settled plan
This is not a re-scope. The design's three chapters are exactly the **surfaces** the settled B→C→D→E order already anticipated:

- **A lot of it already ships and works today.** The album page itself, the full-screen photo viewer (with author-only delete, caption editing, "add it again with sound"), the on-device face dots, the honest video/upload chips, the one-true importer, the self-healing day/place filing, the cross-device live repaint, and the day-scrub strip precedent — all built, all live. For these, Stage E is a **reskin, not a rebuild**, and the real risk is *quietly dropping* shipped behavior (see §4).
- **"Finish the story" (Ch2) is almost entirely doors onto engines that already exist.** Keeping a day gold, pooling quiet days, the nothing-day keep, the pin/settle machinery — all of it already works for *any* date. What's missing is only the *door* that points those engines at a past day or trip. That's genuinely small glue, as the design claims.
- **The parts that wait on B/C/D are the ones the plan already gates.** "Photo moves" (Ch3) is real only once **Stage B's migration 017** lands (I confirmed the newest migration is 016 — 017 does not exist yet), and the whole-trip catch-up and backfill "letter" are hollow until **Stage C** (locating old photos) and **Stage D** (the shadow matcher) have run. This is consistent with the plan, not a surprise.

## 3. The genuinely-NEW things — the honest callouts
These were **not** in the A→E plan and each is a real decision, not a settled detail:

- **Best-of / photo quality scoring** (clarity, exposure, light, composition). I confirmed **no photo-quality scoring exists anywhere today** — the only photo intelligence we have is on-device face recognition. Ranking "best shots" is brand-new engineering, and it is what the "✧ Best" filter and the per-person "best of you" shelf both stand on. *New because: it's net-new scope with no precedent to lean on.*
- **The Claude-vision consent tier — sending photos off the device to be scored.** This is the load-bearing privacy decision. Every bit of photo intelligence today stays **strictly on the device**; nothing leaves. The composition/lighting tier of best-of would need to send family photos out to be analyzed, behind a one-time adults-only opt-in (off / quietly-learning / on). *New because: it opens a first-ever off-device path for family photos — a privacy call only you should make, separate from whether best-of ships at all.*
- **The full album navigation rebuild over a working page.** The new fixed top bar, caption search, "Find" index sheet, filter row, and sticky headers are bolted onto a page that already carries offline rendering, live cross-device repaint, and viewer-survival logic the design never mentions. *New because: it's the highest reconcile-before-replace risk — a naive rebuild silently breaks offline photos and live updates.*
- **Best-of "keep/remove" as shared family taste.** Promoting or demoting a shot *for everyone* needs new synced storage — which is precisely what **migration 017** is for. It cannot ride existing storage. *New because: it's blocked on Stage B and must not be assumed "free."*
- Smaller new pieces: an "N new since this morning" line (needs a per-device last-seen marker), Aurelia choosing "the day's picture," a durable record of *which* quiet days were pooled together, and offering a book page *in the same gesture* as keeping a day (which collides with the "a kept page is frozen" rule and must be reconciled).

## 4. The do-not-lose inventory (a reskin must preserve all of these)
- **Offline photos still render** after relaunch (not dead blobs).
- **Live cross-device repaint** — another phone's photo, note, or delete updates an open album.
- **The viewer survives deletes** — pruning several photos in a row doesn't eject you.
- **Author-only permission boundary** on delete / caption / date — worker-enforced; a visual pass must not loosen it. (Ch3's "any adult can Move, but delete stays author-only" is a *new, different* gate, not this one relaxed.)
- **"Add it again — the sound will come along" door** and the iOS rule that the real file input must be **tapped directly, never scripted open**.
- **Honest video/upload chips** driven by the live upload queue — tiles must never read "saved" while an upload is pending; Rafa's lens stays gentle (no amber).
- **The self-healing filing spine** — place-leads-the-day, chronological fallback for in-transit/unfiled, share-moment de-duplication. Already shipped; do not re-derive.
- **Face data stays on-device** — new person-filter and best-of consumers must not create an off-device face path.
- **The day-scrub sticky/scroll Safari fix** — reuse it, don't rediscover it.
- **The settle machinery deliberately kept OUT of the after-trip keepsake home** — the new invite card *adds* to the photo-wall/relive home; it must not resurrect the evening card.
- **The rich settle-sheet behavior** (covered-place hiding, per-pin leave-out/put-back, who-corrections, Rafa note playback, surprise-safe masking) — a relaid-out past-day page must reuse this kernel, not re-derive it.
- **Counts must respect surprise-masking** — a total must never leak a hidden surprise.

## 5. The one or two sequencing calls that are genuinely yours
Everything above is either settled or mechanical. These two are real forks:

1. **Best-of: on-device only, or on-device *plus* the off-device Claude-vision tier?** You can ship a best-of that ranks purely on the device (sharpness, faces, and who's featured) with **no photo ever leaving** — and treat the composition/lighting vision tier as a separate, later, opt-in decision. Or you commit to the vision path now and design the consent gate up front. This is the privacy fork, and it's cleanly separable — the album's filters and shelves work either way. **Which side do you want to build first?**

2. **Where does Stage E start — the reskin, or the new capabilities?** The lowest-risk, no-new-plumbing start is **Chapter 2 ("finish the story")**: it's almost pure doors onto engines that already work and needs neither migration 017 nor the matcher. The album reskin (Ch1) can proceed in parallel *except* for the best-of shelf and the synced taste override, which wait on the scoring decision (#1) and on Stage B. Chapter 3 ("photo moves") genuinely cannot be wired for real until Stage B ships. **Do you want Ch2 to lead Stage E while B/C/D finish, or hold all of E until the plumbing is fully in?**
