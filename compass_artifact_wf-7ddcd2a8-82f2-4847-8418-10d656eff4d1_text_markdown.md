# Road trip restaurant links: Massachusetts to Texas

Every restaurant on your family road trip itinerary has been researched for verified menu and Yelp photo links. **One critical finding: Surreal Creamery in Dallas appears to be permanently closed** — the chain has pulled out of Texas entirely and now only operates in the Northeast. You'll want to swap that stop. All other 26 restaurants are confirmed open with links below.

For restaurants without a traditional online menu (small bakeries, cash-only spots), alternatives and notes are provided so you don't end up with dead links.

- **Le Rêve Gelato** (Dallas, TX) — Instagram @lerevegp for current pastries and gelato
- **Samad's Cafe** (Dallas, TX) — No web presence; daily specials rotate by day of week (see notes above)
