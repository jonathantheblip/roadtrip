# Saturday, April 18, 2026 — Actual Route

**Route:** Postcard Cabins (Catskill, NY) → Elizabethton, TN
**Total driving:** ~14 hours
**Total door-to-door:** ~16.5 hours (morning departure → ~12:25 AM arrival)

## Who was where

All four: Jonathan, Helen, Aurelia, Rafa. Helen and Jonathan traded off driving hourly from the WV welcome center onward.

## Stops, in order

### Morning — Fred's Coffee
Breakfast, Catskills area. Out the door to Scranton.

### Steamtown National Historic Site — Scranton, PA
Rafa's anchor — working steam locomotives, big machines. Jonathan appreciated the rail engineering. Aurelia tolerated it; Helen used the time for a walk.

### Breaker Brewing — Wilkes-Barre, PA
Lunch. Confirmed open. Helen's vegetarian needs were met here — this is why we picked it over roadside options.

### LeTort Park — Carlisle, PA
Rafa's run-around stop before the long afternoon stretch. Playground, open space. ~30 min.

### West Virginia Welcome Center — I-81 S
Brief stop, 5:21 PM. Swapped drivers. Confirmed the dinner target.

### Box Office Brewery — Strasburg, VA (~6:15 PM)
1918 Strand Theater, converted to brewpub. Called ahead about Saturday night live music — asked for off-stage seating.

*Chosen over Roma Casual (Stephens City) because we'd already done Italian the night before at Savona's Hudson. Aesthetic novelty won over Roma's stronger vegetarian menu. Box Office had Beyond Burger + build-your-own pizza for Helen.*

Aurelia loved the 1933-34 film posters on the walls and the old main stage. Rafa got pizza. Helen got the Beyond Burger. Jonathan got a flight.

### Buc-ee's — Roanoke area
Gas and snacks.

### Mill Mountain Star — Roanoke, VA (~8:55 PM)
88-ft neon star, panoramic night view of Roanoke. Aurelia's photo stop. 15 minutes. Worth the detour.

### I-26 E → Elizabethton
Final 3h 15m stretch. Rafa asleep early in this leg. Aurelia asleep by Johnson City.

### Arrived — 317 E Cottage Ave, Elizabethton TN
~12:25 AM EDT.

## Meals

- **Breakfast:** Fred's Coffee (Catskills)
- **Lunch:** Breaker Brewing (Wilkes-Barre)
- **Dinner:** Box Office Brewery (Strasburg)

## What worked

- **Mill Mountain Star** earned its slot. A short vivid moment for Aurelia at night.
- **Box Office** was the right call on novelty — eating Italian two nights running would have dulled the week.
- **Breaker for lunch** kept Helen fed well; cascaded into an easier dinner choice.

## What we learned

- The Savona's / Roma's comparison is a real pattern. Novelty matters more than menu depth when the family is eating three restaurant meals in 36 hours.
- 14 hours of driving is the ceiling, not a baseline. Tomorrow (Sunday) is 8 hours — deliberately shorter.
- Mid-afternoon driver swap was the right call. Helen took Carlisle → Roanoke, Jonathan took Roanoke → Elizabethton.

## Sunday morning

Ceremony. No wheels-up before 10 AM ET. This is the only fixed point of Sunday morning and is not to be scheduled over.
