# Jonathan — Podcast Listening Queue

General listening queue for the road trip and beyond. Not tied to route locations. Show in Jonathan's Media tab only. All links are Overcast.

## Currently listening / retry

- **The Silt Verses** — https://overcast.fm/itunes1547222295 — Commercialized gods, outlawed river worship, and the most inventive mythology in audio drama. Retry from ep 1.
- **Stellar Firma** — https://overcast.fm/itunes1451762036 — Semi-improvised sci-fi comedy from the Magnus Archives team; incompetent planet designer + desperate clone assistant. Retry from ep 1.

## New queue — format-forward picks (start here)

- **Within the Wires** — https://overcast.fm/itunes1121391184 — Each season is a different found-audio format (relaxation tapes, museum guides, voicemails) from an alternate dystopia. Night Vale co-creator. Give it 3 eps.
- **SAYER** — https://overcast.fm/itunes831100527 — A corporate AI on a moon base addresses YOU in second person. The format is the horror. Give it 2–3 eps.
- **Dreamboy** — https://overcast.fm/itunes1437904233 — Musical surrealist audio drama where inner thoughts become original songs. Cosmic horror + queer romance. Only 8 episodes. NSFW. Give it 2 eps.
- **Kakos Industries** — https://overcast.fm/itunes823315247 — Evil megacorp shareholder announcements. Night Vale's format crossed with Venture Brothers' tone. Give it 2–3 eps.
- **Death by Dying** — https://overcast.fm/itunes1437812269 — Every episode is an obituary for the fictional town of Crestfall, Idaho. Dark comedy mystery told backward from confirmed deaths. Give it 2–3 eps.
- **Camp Here & There** — https://overcast.fm/itunes1566268240 — Summer camp PA announcements that cheerfully cover meal schedules and supernatural terrors in the same breath. Will Wood soundtrack. Give it 3–4 eps.
- **Moonbase Theta, Out** — https://overcast.fm/itunes1439047722 — Five-minute weekly reports from the last guy on a decommissioning moonbase. Micro-format, massive emotional compression. Give it 5 eps (25 min total).

## New queue — anthologies (for The Program fans)

- **The Truth** — https://overcast.fm/itunes502304410 — Gold standard standalone fiction anthology. Movies for your ears. Pick any well-rated episode. Give it 1–3 eps.
- **Zero Hours** — https://overcast.fm/itunes1483241955 — Seven standalone end-of-the-world stories set 99 years apart. From the Wolf 359 team. Only 7 episodes. Give it 1–2 eps.
- **Knifepoint Horror** — https://overcast.fm/itunes406250030 — Radical minimalist horror. One narrator, no music, no effects. Start with "staircase" or "penpal." Give it 1 ep.

## New queue — completed narratives

- **Steal the Stars** — https://overcast.fm/itunes1259505930 — Noir sci-fi heist in first person. Security chief plans to steal a crashed alien body from her own facility. 14 episodes. Give it 2–3 eps.
- **The Hyacinth Disaster** — https://overcast.fm/itunes1321203327 — Black box recordings from a doomed mining vessel. Seven episodes, devastating. Give it 1–2 eps.

## New queue — literary / slow burn

- **Mabel** — https://overcast.fm/itunes1160860118 — Voicemails from a home health aide that crack open into folklore horror and a love story. Poetic, deliberately opaque. Give it 3–4 eps.
- **Palimpsest** — https://overcast.fm/itunes1288069795 — Audio journal literary horror. Each season is a self-contained arc in a different setting. Elegant prose, psychological terror. Give it 2–3 eps.

## New queue — long shot

- **WOE.BEGONE** — https://overcast.fm/itunes1542792309 — Podcast-within-a-podcast about a violent online ARG involving time travel. Unique original score every episode. Answers questions instead of vamping, but 235+ episodes deep. Give it 3–5 eps.

## Previously recommended, not yet tried

- **Ars Paradoxica** — https://overcast.fm/itunes1006173000 — Physicist thrown back to 1940s America; time travel as both physics problem and political weapon. Completed, 3 seasons. Give it 3 eps.
- **I Am In Eskew** — https://overcast.fm/itunes1339770338 — Man trapped in a nightmarish, impossible city records everything. From the Silt Verses creators. 25 episodes, no filler. Give it 2 eps.
